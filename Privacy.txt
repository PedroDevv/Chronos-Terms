Privacy Policy for AI Chat Bot "Chronos" on Discord

Effective Date: 07/05/24

Welcome to Chronos, our AI Chat bot on Discord! This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with Chronos, powered by Meta's LLaMA AI model. Please read this policy carefully. By accessing or using Chronos, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information (PII) from users interacting with Chronos on Discord. Chronos utilizes Meta's LLaMA AI model to process and respond to messages within the Discord platform. Any data processed by Chronos is solely for the purpose of providing and improving the chatbot experience.

2. Use of Your Information

Chronos does not retain conversations or store personal data beyond what is necessary for the current chat session. The AI model processes messages in real-time and does not maintain a memory of past interactions or user profiles.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not disclose or transfer any personal information collected by Chronos to third parties unless required by law or as necessary to protect our rights. Discord may collect and process certain information as described in Discord's own Privacy Policy.

4. Security of Your Information

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the confidentiality and security of information transmitted to and from Chronos on Discord. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Chronos is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will promptly delete that information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our Discord server, "The Chronos Foundation". You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or Chronos on Discord, please visit our Discord server, "The Chronos Foundation", or contact us via Discord direct messages.
